Hi! I am Dr. Prateek Raj Gautam.

I have earned my Ph.D. in 2022 from Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology at Allahabad, Prayagraj, India. under the supervision of Dr. Arvind Kumar (Associate Professor ECED, MNNIT) on the topic "Energy-Efficient 2D and 3D Localization in Wireless Sensor Networks using Single Anchor Node."

I have around 4 years of pre-PhD teaching experience as an Assistant Professor at Allen house Institute of Technology and Naraina College of Engineering and Technology.

I have received M.Tech. degree in electronics and communication engineering from Harcourt Butler Technological Institute Kanpur, India, in 2011.

My research interests are in the energy-efficient scheme for wireless sensor networks, image processing, CDMA, IDMA, and brain wave mapping.

I have advanced experience with subject-related software like MATLAB, Python, CST Studio Suite, LTspice.

I have sound skills at producing high-quality documents and illustrations in LaTeX/Tikz and 2d and 3d drawing and animation software like Inkscape, GIMP, and Blender.

I would like to research in the field of wireless networks and their application. Currently, I am exploring optimization and machine learning and its applications in Wireless Sensor Networks.
