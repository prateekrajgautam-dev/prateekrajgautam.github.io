Introduction

Hi! I am Dr. Prateek Raj Gautam.

I earned my Ph.D. in 2022 from Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad, Prayagraj, India. under the supervision of Dr. Arvind Kumar (Associate Professor ECED, MNNIT) on the topic "Energy-Efficient 2D and 3D Localization in Wireless Sensor Networks using Single Anchor Node."

I have 4 years teaching experience as an Assistant Professor at Allen house Institute of Technology and Naraina College of Engineering and Technology.

I have received M.Tech. degree in electronics and communication engineering from Harcourt Butler Technological Institute Kanpur, India, in 2011.

My research interests are in the energy-efficient scheme for wireless sensor networks, internet of things, image processing, CDMA, IDMA, and brain wave mapping.

I have advanced experience with subject-related software like MATLAB, Python, CST Studio Suite, LTspice.

I have good skills at producing high-quality documents and illustrations in LaTeX/Tikz and 2d and 3d drawing and animation software like Inkscape, GIMP, and Blender.

I have interent in web and desktop app development using html, css, js, python, django, kivy, eel, and wordpress .

I would like to continue research in the field of wireless networks and their application. Currently, I am exploring optimization and machine learning and its applications in Wireless Sensor Networks.

Please find the attached reaume for your consideration.
